**INTERNAL REPORT: JOHN F. KENNEDY INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT (JFK)**\
**Prepared by:** Northeastern Aviation Oversight Bureau\
**Date:** August 5, 2025\
**CONFIDENTIAL - FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY**

---

### 1. General Information

* **Airport Name:** John F. Kennedy International Airport
* **Airport Code:** JFK
* **Location:** Queens, New York City, NY, USA
* **Ownership:** Port Authority of New York and New Jersey (PANYNJ)
* **Operator:** PANYNJ
* **FAA Classification:** Large Hub Primary Commercial Service Airport

---

### 2. Operational Hours

* **Open:** 24 hours / 7 days a week
* **Peak Operating Hours:**

  * Morning: 6:00 AM - 10:00 AM
  * Evening: 5:00 PM - 9:00 PM
* **Customs & Immigration:**

  * Staffed 24/7 at Terminals 1, 4, 5, 7, and 8
* **Curfew/Noise Restrictions:**

  * Voluntary nighttime quiet hours (midnight-6 AM), especially for cargo and private flights

---

### 3. Capacity & Infrastructure

* **Passenger Capacity:**

  * Annual Maximum Design: \~85 million passengers
  * Current Annual Throughput: \~65 million
* **Aircraft Movements:**

  * Daily Average: \~1,300
  * Runways: 4 (13L/31R, 13R/31L, 4L/22R, 4R/22L)
* **Terminals:**

  * 6 operational terminals (Terminals 1, 4, 5, 7, 8, and the new Terminal 6 under construction)
  * Gates: Approx. 130 total

---

### 4. Personnel

* **Total Staff on Premises:** \~45,000 (including airline, TSA, security, customs, retail)
* **Security Force:**

  * TSA Agents: \~2,100
  * CBP Officers: \~350
  * NYPD Aviation Unit Presence: On-site support
* **Emergency Services:**

  * On-site Fire Department Division
  * 24/7 paramedic teams and mobile trauma units

---

### 5. Airlines & Routes

* **Number of Airlines:** 70+ domestic and international carriers
* **Top Carriers:** Delta Air Lines, American Airlines, JetBlue (Terminal 5 hub), British Airways, Emirates
* **Direct Destinations:**

  * Domestic: 80+ cities
  * International: 100+ cities worldwide
* **Cargo Carriers:** FedEx, DHL, UPS, Atlas Air, Cargolux

---

### 6. Ground Transport & Access

* **Public Transit Access:**

  * AirTrain JFK (connecting terminals, parking, and subway/rail)
  * NYC Subway (E, J, Z lines via Jamaica Station; A line via Howard Beach)
  * LIRR (Long Island Rail Road) via Jamaica Station
* **Parking:** 17,500+ spaces (short-term, long-term, economy lots)
* **Rental Cars:** 12 agencies located off-site, connected via AirTrain and shuttle
* **Taxis and Rideshare:** Available 24/7; flat rate to Manhattan (\$70 as of 2025)

---

### 7. Key Features

* **Customs & Border Protection (CBP):**

  * Biometric facial recognition systems at all international terminals
* **Baggage Handling:**

  * Fully integrated multi-terminal baggage tracking (RFID-enabled for major carriers)
* **VIP & Private Flights:**

  * Signature Flight Support and Sheltair handle private aviation services
* **Lounges:** Over 20 airline and premium lounges

---

### 8. Ongoing & Future Projects

* **Ongoing (2025):**

  * Construction of New Terminal 6 (connecting with Terminal 5)
  * Terminal 1 modernization project
  * Taxiway realignment to reduce congestion
* **Planned (2026-2028):**

  * Runway resurfacing (13R/31L)
  * Expansion of Cargo Zone B
  * Integration with proposed NYC-JFK High-Speed Rail line (pending approval)
