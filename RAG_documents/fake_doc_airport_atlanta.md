**INTERNAL REPORT: ATLANTA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT (ATL)**\
**Prepared by:** Regional Operations Bureau\
**Date:** August 5, 2025\
**CONFIDENTIAL - FOR INTERNAL USE ONLY**

---

### 1. General Information

* **Airport Name:** Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport
* **Airport Code:** ATL
* **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, USA
* **Ownership:** City of Atlanta
* **Operator:** Atlanta Department of Aviation
* **FAA Classification:** Large Hub Primary Commercial Service Airport

---

### 2. Operational Hours

* **Open:** 24 hours / 7 days a week
* **Peak Operating Hours:**

  * Morning Rush: 5:30 AM - 9:00 AM
  * Evening Rush: 4:30 PM - 7:30 PM
* **Customs and Immigration Services:**

  * Open Daily: 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM

---

### 3. Capacity & Infrastructure

* **Passenger Capacity:**

  * Annual Throughput: \~110 million passengers (max operational design)
  * Average Daily Passenger Volume: \~300,000
* **Aircraft Movements:**

  * Daily Average: \~2,500 flights
  * Runways: 5 parallel runways (9L/27R, 9R/27L, 8L/26R, 8R/26L, 10/28)
* **Terminal Information:**

  * Concourses: 7 (T, A, B, C, D, E, F)
  * Gates: 195 total
  * International Terminal: Concourse F

---

### 4. Staffing & Personnel

* **Airport Staff:** Approx. 63,000 (including airline, TSA, customs, and support staff)
* **Security Personnel:**

  * TSA Agents: 2,500
  * CBP Officers: 400
* **Emergency Services:**

  * Fire & Rescue Stations: 3 on-site units
  * Medical: 24/7 staffed clinic with ambulance service

---

### 5. Airlines & Destinations

* **Number of Airlines Operating:** 38
* **Top Carriers:** Delta Air Lines (hub), Southwest, American Airlines
* **Direct Destinations:**

  * Domestic: 150+ cities
  * International: 75+ cities across 5 continents

---

### 6. Ground Transportation & Parking

* **Parking Capacity:** 33,000 vehicles (short-term, long-term, economy)
* **Rental Car Center:** 13 rental agencies connected via SkyTrain
* **Public Transit Access:**

  * MARTA Rail (Red and Gold Lines) - Direct airport station
  * Shuttle Services: 24/7 to downtown and hotels

---

### 7. Notable Features

* **Baggage Handling System:** Fully automated system with RFID tracking
* **Sustainability Initiatives:**

  * LEED-certified buildings
  * Solar panel fields and electric ground service vehicles
* **VIP/Private Services:**

  * Two fixed-base operators (FBOs) for private jets
  * VIP lounge and concierge services available

---

### 8. Recent & Upcoming Projects

* **2024 Completed:**

  * Expansion of Concourse T (5 new gates)
  * Upgrade of Terminal F security checkpoint
* **Planned 2025-2026:**

  * New Intermodal Transportation Hub
  * Expansion of International Arrivals Hall
  * Runway 9R/27L resurfacing project
